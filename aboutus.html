Classically speaking, our BOOKSTORE is...

...a company that was founded in 2011 and deals with the distribution of books, textbooks and professional magazines, as well as retailing of books in English in Terezija BB Street (Centar Skenderija - Privredni grad) in Sarajevo. Since 2012, BOOKSTORE has been a partner of the publishing house Macmillan Education UK in the distribution and co-publishing of textbooks for learning English. In addition to Macmillan Education books, books.ba also distributes publications from over 200 foreign publishing houses.

And in reality, our BOOKSTORE is...

...a dynamic and passionate team of book lovers, with the desire to entertain, inform, educate and improve the life skills of both ourselves and our visitors with a quality and affordable offer and eternal hope for a better tomorrow!
